myShopDash for Everyday Rewards

Privacy Policy

for myShoppingData browser extension and myShoppingDash viewer

1. Hey!

Here is some important information on how this service handle your information.

The Service includes the website https://myShopDash.appspot.com (the Website) and the complimentary Chrome Browser Extension “myShoppingData for Everyday Rewards” (the Extension). The Service is used for downloading, compiling, decoding and visualising the data from the User’s Everyday Award account (also known as Woolworths Award program).

2. At a glance

The Service has been created with no intention of collecting personal data. That’s why it doesn’t ask for your name, e-mail, address, phone number, login details, credit card, neither does it collect those from your Everyday Rewards Account.

While the Service has access to the information about your buys in Woolworths Supermarkets and other retail outlets, this information is generally stored only for the duration of your interactions with the Service and always for no longer than 36 hours.

The creator of this service is not in any way affiliated with Woolworths (except for being a loyal customer).

3. What types of data we can collect and how

i. Client-side (the Extension)

The Extension runs when the user is logged in to their Everyday Account. It downloads user’s shopping statements and eReceipts, including dates, addresses of stores, items bought, the amount paid, the number of the loyalty card presented, mode of payment, four last digits of the number of the bank card used as payment, collected/spent loyalty points. The Extension requests this data from the program servers and stores it locally in the user’s browser.

The User can choose to download this data onto their computer directly from the browser. In this case, the data is not transferred anywhere.

ii. Server-side (the Website)

The User can choose to upload data to the Website, which provides convenient tools for analysing, decoding and reviewing the data. In this case, the information will be stored in the Website data storage for the duration of the user session.

The data will be accessible only to the user that uploaded it and only from their current browser. The user can choose to delete the data from the server by pressing the ‘Delete data’ button. Otherwise, the user’s files are deleted no later than 36 hours of uploading into the service.

4. Data protection measures

Neither the Extension nor the Service ask you your login and password for your Everyday Reward account. You need to log in directly on the Everyday Reward site and run the Extension within your genuine logged session. As such, the Service does not have access and can not store your login and password.

The Service doesn’t query user’s personal information from the Everyday Rewards API.

The Internet connection between your computer and the Service is encrypted with modern encryption algorithms.

The Service doesn’t provide your information to third parties.

In exceptional circumstances, internet services may be legally required to disclose information about their user’s interactions. However, the Service maintains very limited access to user’s information and stores it for a very short time.

5. Technical data

For the purpose of maintenance, technical stability and security, the Website may use standard industrial mechanisms, such as logging data about network requests. It only stores information that your browser routinely sends to external servers, such as IP address, date and time of the request, time zone, requested page address, HTTP status code, the amount of data transferred in each case, the website from which the request originates, browser name and version, language setting.

The Website uses cookies to control access to your information. The cookie is stored on your computer for the duration of your access session and is automatically deleted when you close your browser.

You can audit the claims about data handling practices in the Service in its source code published on GitHub: github.com/ekutilov/wooliesR.
