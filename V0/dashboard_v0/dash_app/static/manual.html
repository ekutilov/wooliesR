About this site

This site is a very simple visual dashboard that allows you to review your shopping data from Everyday Rewards loyalty program (previously known as Woolworths Rewards - only in Australia). You probably know, that if you opt-in, you can see all your eReceipts from Woolworths, BigW and BWS on the Everyday Rewards (ER) website and in the ER mobile app. You only can see individual receipts though, being unable to download all your data in a machine readable format - i.e. Excel.

With my dashboard it is possible!

It is just a little lockdown hobby project (and my name is Eugene btw). I like data and like making it accessible. Hope, you'll enjoy it.

This site is not affiliated with Woolworths. All thademarks belong they respective owners. It is not intended to infringe IP or data security arrangements at Woolworths. It uses a publicly available API access point, and does nothing any user can't do with a standard web browser. A disclamer about user's data privacy is here.

Why

You may need aggregated info about your supermarket buys for different purposes:

  1. You may see how, where and what you spend most of your groceries expences on. Probably, want to optimise it, like buying things in bulk.

  2. You may see prices on individual products and how they change in time.

  3. You may have a separate loyalty account for your taxable expences and you can export you expences directly to your accounting software.

  4. You may save your data for future analysis, as eReceipts on Everyday Rewards website are saved only for 14 months, and you may wish to see a deeper data horizon.

How to use it

Download Extension

Install myShopData for Everyday Rewards Chrome extension from Chrome Web Store.

Navigate to Everyday Rewards

Open your account's activity page at the Everyday Rewards website, log in with your login, password or one-time code.

Launch the Extension

Click the Extension's icon in your browser's extension shelf, provide nessesary permissions.

Follow prompts

Follow the prompts in the extension's interface to complete the downloading process.

Open data in Viewer

Finally, at the last screen click 'Open Viewer' if you want your data opened in myShopDash dashboard.

Install Chrome Extension

You will need to use specialised free Chrome Extension: myShopData. It is available only for Google Chrome at this stage and available from official Chrome Web Store. It will securely bulk-download your eReceipts for you and help to send this data to myShopDash in one file.

Use the Extension on the Everyday Rewards website

When installed, navigate to Everyday Rewards website and log in there. Then run the Extension by pressing the button in your browser's extension shelf.

Save your data or send here

The Extension guides you through the process. You will be able to choose from two options: save your data locally as an encoded file (for experts: it is actually a json file with all the information API provides) or upload it onto myShopDash in one click. The Extension does not send your data to this website until you press the button, so you can choose to save your data securely on your computer without sending it to myShopDash if neccessary.

View your data

If you choose to upload your data on myShopDash: you will be redirected onto your dashboard immediately. Your data will be temporarily saved on this server. Nobody will be able to see your data except for you. Your view will be accessible only from your current browser and only for a limited time (no longer than 36 hours and only until your browser window is open).

myShopDash decode the data, summarise and visualise it. If you have never used it, you can see it in action in demo-mode here (a depersonalised demonstration data file is used for this demo-version).

From your dashboard you can download your decoded data on your computer as Excel file.

Delete your data from myShopDash

You can delete your data from the server at any time by pressing the red button at the bottom of the page.

If you navidated from the Dashboard very recently and didn't deleted your data, you can return back to the dashboard here and continue your session.

If you forget to delete your data, myShopDash deletes it automatically no later than 36 hours after your visit.

Features and issues

It is very likely that the service will not work exactly as described, especially at first. I will be grateful for detailed bug reports, so bugs can be fixed. You can also suggest fixes directly through GitHub.

The most typical expected problem, that your eReceipts are not parced/decoded properly, so they are shown corrupted or break the dashboard completely. If this happens, consider sending me your JSON file, downloaded directly from myShopData Extension. I can guarantee confidentiality of your shopping details.

New features will be being added as we go, and any suggestions are appreciated.

As myShopDash (or, rather, the browser extension) uses a Woolworths' corporate program interface that is not oficially documented, some issues might arise if modification to the protocol are introduced by the company. So if something looks broken, drop me a message.
